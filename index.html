Start The Cycle

The Bike Library



Borrow a bike like you borrow a book
 
 
Start the Cycle is a non-profit organization with the strategic goal of developing a cycling culture from the ground up. By targeting youth we can develop a new generation of cyclists that otherwise might have never existed.
 


Choose a Bike & Helmet
from a range of sizes in the catalogue.

Check it out
from the librarian to get your key.

Unlock & and Go
use the bike for up to 48 hours.

Return
the bike, helmet, and key during operational hours.
